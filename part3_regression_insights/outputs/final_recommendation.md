5. Risks and Limitations

The regression model has several limitations:

Regression identifies statistical relationships but does not prove cause-and-effect.
External factors such as seasonality, competition, pricing strategy, economic conditions, and customer preferences were not included in the model.
Future business conditions may differ from historical data, reducing prediction accuracy.
Some variables may influence each other, which can affect coefficient interpretation.
6. Why Regression Shows Association but Not Causation

Regression analysis measures the strength of relationships between variables. A significant relationship does not necessarily mean that one variable directly causes another.

For example, higher marketing spend may be associated with higher sales, but sales can also be influenced by factors such as promotions, product quality, store location, and customer demand. Therefore, regression results should be used together with business knowledge and management experience before making strategic decisions.
